Gathered friends, listen again to our legend of the BIONICLE...


Beginning in February 2019, Gathered Friends is a read-along book club podcast covering all of BIONICLE’s written history. Appearing on 810NICLE Day 3 times over its original 2 season run, (and 1 special) and spawning 3 spinoff shows, Gathered Friends featured new and familiar faces alike from throughout the community, as well as a handful of special guests including Greg Farshtey and Ryder Windham, writers of the G1 and G2 novels respectively.

Now on the 5th anniversary of the show, Gathered Friends returns for a third season in 2024! This time around they'll be covering the Myths & Legacy catalogue, along with some other curated fan works. Join Liam, Chip, Levi, Josie, and new host Maks as we revisit the stories of the Great Spirit, now told by YOU.

Unity, Duty, Destiny. This is the way of the BIONICLE.
