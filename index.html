LIVE THE LEGEND


Whether you're new to the series or a longtime fan, there are many different ways you can experience the legend of BIONICLE:


BIOLOGICAL CHRONICLE


Biological Chronicle is one of the first comprehensive resources of digitized BIONICLE bibliography produced by the community. Painstakingly compiled over a period of several years by TuragaNuva and her team at Cross-Wired Geeks, Biological Chronicle contains everything from books, comics, and transcribed game content compiled into conveniently grouped e-books. This project, (or the 2019 complete version of it) contains the content that we read for season 1 of the show. If you want to directly read along with us from the very beginning, Biological Chronicle would be your best choice.

Learn more at: crosswiredgeeks.com



WALL OF HISTORY


Created by JSLBrowning, Wall of History is another fantastic resource for the written works of BIONICLE. In contrast to Biological Chronicle, Wall of History is entirely web based, with a very user-friendly interface crammed full of options to improve and customize the reading experience to your liking. Wall of History also contains many other pieces of the BIONICLE story including movies, video games, character bios, and more, making it one of the best resources to experience everything that BIONICLE has to offer. While it may not perfectly align exactly with the reading order and material of Gathered Friends, it is still a perfectly viable option to catch up on the story while you listen.

Learn more at: wallofhistory.com



MYTHS & LEGACY


Spawned from the chatrooms of Custom BIONICLE Wiki, Myths & Legacy was created by Gonel and BobTheDoctor27 as a hub of curated, canon-compliant fan fiction to expand on the lore of the BIONICLE legend, as well as fill in its many missing pieces. Now probably most well-known for the Vision of the Great Beings project co-helmed by Greg Farshtey himself, Myths & Legacy plays host to dozens of tales both long and short, written by an ever-expanding team of writers. Season 3 of Gathered Friends covers the entire Myths & Legacy catalogue.

Learn more at: mythsandlegacy.com



THE BIOMEDIA PROJECT


The single largest archive of BIONICLE media on the internet, The BioMedia Project has dutifully curated hundreds of gigabytes of content for well over a decade now. Originally created by Auron on BZPower as simply "The BIONICLE Music Project" and now helmed under Mask Of Destiny, the archives contain everything from images and videos, to style guides and other internal documents. Data that may have once been lost to the well of time has since been carefully curated and preserved for generations to come. If you're looking to engage with, (most) G2 content or to follow along with season 2 of the show, this would be the place to look.

Learn more at: biomediaproject.com
